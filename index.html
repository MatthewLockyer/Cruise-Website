Beaches, backstreets and timeless beauty With beauty waiting at every stop, there’s plenty to explore. Famed for its art, architecture, cultures, cuisines, beaches and more – the Med is a holiday full of rich experiences and it's perfect for adults or families to experience together.

Escape to the Mediterranean on a cruise holiday

Leave the lounger behind and treat the family to a world of eye-opening culture, mouth-watering cuisine and hair-raising adventure with our 7 night and 10 night Mediterranean cruise holidays. Even when the sun goes down, each ship offers a variety of exciting entertainment from established to emerging performers.

Monte Carlo, Monaco

Monte Carlo is famous for the four 'G's': the Grimaldi monarchy as well as its Grand Prix, Glamour and Gambling.

Venice, Italy

Discover this World Heritage City with it's famous canals, narrow cobbled streets and 500 historic bridges within the city centre.

Valencia, Spain

Amongst art and architecture you can see plazas, palaces, churches and cathedrals, before heading to one of the city's white sandy beaches to cool off..

Katakolon, Greece

The small Greek port of Katakolon was developed in the 19th century to serve the thriving local trade in currants. Today it is your starting block for Olympia - birthplace of the Olympic Games.
